About me

Hello! I'm Prateek Bansal, a PhD candidate at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), specializing in computational chemistry. My research focuses on molecular modeling, simulations, and the application of machine learning techniques to solve complex biochemical problems.

I am passionate about education and outreach, having served as the Head Lab Assistant for the CURIE Summer Camp at UIUC.

Feel free to reach out to me via email at pdb3@illinois.edu or connect with me on Google Scholar and LinkedIn.
